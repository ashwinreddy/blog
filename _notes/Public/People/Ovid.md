Publius Ovidius Naso (43 BC - 17/18 AD) was a Roman poet. His first work was _Amores_, three books of love poems. His magnum opus is [[Metamorphoses]]. He was exiled by Augustus. He was also clearly influenced by [[Virgil]] and [[Horace]] and [[Callimachus]].
