Schopenhauer 

[[Germany]]


Schopenhauer had a bit of a feud with [[Hegel]], choosing to have his lectures after Hegel, which were well-attended while his own were neglected.


Schopenhauer thinks [[Music]] is the highest art. 


[[Kant]]
